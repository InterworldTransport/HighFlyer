Background

The High Flyer Application is actually a collection of smaller tools designed to work together. The Utilities package is where most of the miscellaneous pieces are placed. This explains why the event model and gui classes are mixed together under one package.

Classes with a main() method may be called manually. They may also be called by the other tools in the application.
